About Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To preserve, propagate, and promote the cultural heritage of Telangana and to address the social, cultural, and educational needs of the Telangana community.

Our Vision: To see a vibrant, cohesive, and progressive Telangana community in Detroit that preserves its identity while contributing to the broader society.
