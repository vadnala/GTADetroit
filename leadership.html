Our Leadership

The Global Telangana Association Detroit Chapter is led by a dedicated team of volunteers committed to serving our community. Our leadership brings together diverse expertise and a shared passion for preserving and promoting Telangana's rich cultural heritage while fostering unity and progress among our members.
